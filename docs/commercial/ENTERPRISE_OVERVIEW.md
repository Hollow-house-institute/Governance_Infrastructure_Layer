# Governance Infrastructure Layer

## Execution-Time Governance for AI and Operational Systems

---

## The problem

AI systems and automated workflows fail in production for one reason:

Behavior is not controlled at execution.

This creates:

- Behavioral Drift
- Governance Drift
- Decision Substitution
- Escalation Suppression
- Accountability Diffusion

These failures do not appear once.

They accumulate.

This is Longitudinal Risk.

---

## What this system does

This system enforces governance during execution.

- Detects Behavioral Drift in real time  
- Evaluates Decision Boundary conditions  
- Triggers Escalation when Intervention Threshold is crossed  
- Enforces Stop Authority at critical severity  
- Records Governance Telemetry and Interaction Trace  
- Binds Accountability to every resolution  

Execution cannot continue without resolution.

---

## What gets detected

- drift from runtime behavior

## What gets blocked

- execution past Decision Boundary when risk is critical

## What evidence is created

- escalation.json  
- STOP enforcement artifact  
- telemetry logs  
- full Interaction Trace  

## Who resolves it

- explicitly accountable actor  
- recorded and time-bound  

---

## Why this matters

Without enforcement:

- Escalation becomes optional  
- Stop Authority is bypassed  
- Accountability disappears  
- Governance Failure compounds
