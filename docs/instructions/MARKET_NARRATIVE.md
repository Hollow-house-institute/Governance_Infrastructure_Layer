# MARKET NARRATIVE

## Problem

AI systems fail in production because governance is applied after execution.

This creates:
- Behavioral Drift
- Governance Drift
- Decision Substitution
- Escalation Decay
- Accountability Diffusion

## What this product changes

This system enforces Execution-Time Governance:

- detects Behavioral Drift
- evaluates Decision Boundary conditions
- triggers Escalation at Intervention Threshold
- enforces Stop Authority at critical severity
- records Interaction Trace
- outputs Governance Telemetry
- binds Accountability to resolution

## What gets detected

- drift conditions from runtime behavior

## What gets blocked

- execution past Decision Boundary when severity is critical

## What evidence is generated

- escalation.json
- STOP artifact
- telemetry logs
- Interaction Trace

## Who resolves it

- accountable actor explicitly recorded

## How this reduces Longitudinal Risk

It stops accumulation of unresolved drift over time.

## Why buyers care

### CTO
Prevents uncontrolled execution

### Risk
Makes Longitudinal Risk visible before failure

### Legal
Creates defensible evidence of intervention

### Audit
Provides full Interaction Trace

### CFO
Reduces cost from repeated governance failure

## Position

This is not monitoring.

This is control.

This is Governance as Infrastructure.
