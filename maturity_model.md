# Governance Infrastructure Maturity Model

## Level 1 — Post-Hoc Governance
- No enforcement
- No Decision Boundary
- No Accountability binding
- Governance Illusion present

## Level 2 — Observability
- Behavioral Drift detected
- Governance Telemetry present
- No enforcement

## Level 3 — Partial Control
- Decision Boundary defined
- Escalation triggered inconsistently
- Stop Authority not enforced reliably

## Level 4 — Execution-Time Governance
- Behavioral Drift enforced
- Decision Boundary active
- Escalation non-optional
- Stop Authority enforced
- Interaction Trace complete
- Accountability bound

## Level 5 — Governance as Infrastructure
- Continuous Assurance active
- Longitudinal Accountability preserved
- No Escalation Suppression
- No Accountability Diffusion
- No Authority Drift
- System is audit-defensible

## Advancement Requirement

Progression requires:
- enforcement, not description
- persistence of control over time
- complete Interaction Trace
- validated Governance Telemetry
